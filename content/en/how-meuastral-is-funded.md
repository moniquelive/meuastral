MeuAstral is free to use. The site may be funded by advertising, including ads served through Google AdSense.

We do not charge for readings, sell spiritual consultations, or hide paid recommendations inside editorial content.

Advertising helps cover hosting, development, content review, and technical maintenance.

## How ads are handled

The site may load advertising scripts only on pages eligible for ads. Pages such as contact, privacy policy, and who maintains MeuAstral may be marked internally with `ads: false` so the ad script does not load.

Optional cookie consent starts denied. When a visitor accepts, ad and measurement services may use cookies according to their own policies. When a visitor rejects, the interactive reading remains available.

## Editorial separation

Ads do not define the symbolic interpretation on pages, do not change the methodology, and do not turn editorial content into paid recommendations. Relevant monetization changes should be reflected on this page or in the [privacy policy](/en/privacy-policy/).
