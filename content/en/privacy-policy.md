MeuAstral uses browser local storage to remember the birth date you choose. This information stays on your device and is used to personalize the interactive reading.

## Data saved in the browser

The interactive reading may save the selected date in `user-birthday`, in your browser local storage. This data is not used to create an account, does not require login, and can be removed by clearing browser data.

Optional cookie preferences are saved in `meuastral.cookieConsent`. That record stores only the banner choice and the update date.

## Cookies, ads, and measurement

By default, MeuAstral sets Google ad and measurement consent to denied. If you accept optional cookies, services such as Google AdSense and audience measurement may use cookies or identifiers according to their own policies.

You can change your choice through the “Cookie preferences” link in the page footer. Rejecting optional cookies does not block the main interactive reading, but it may limit personalized ads and audience statistics.

## What we do not do

MeuAstral does not sell personal readings, does not ask for sensitive personal data, and does not require payment information for free readings.
