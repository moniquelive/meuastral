---

MeuAstral uses browser local storage to remember the birth date you choose. This information stays on your device and is used to personalize the interactive reading.

The site may display advertising through Google AdSense and may use audience measurement tools. Those services can use cookies or identifiers according to their own policies.

MeuAstral does not sell personal readings, does not ask for sensitive personal data, and does not require payment information for free readings.
