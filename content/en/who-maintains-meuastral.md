MeuAstral is an independent editorial project created to offer free symbolic readings combining horoscope, biorhythm, and ascended masters. Maintenance includes content, technology, editorial review, and transparency about advertising.

## Editorial responsibility

The site is maintained as a self-knowledge and conscious entertainment tool. Each page should make clear when content is symbolic, when an external source is integrated, and when the final decision depends on the reader.

MeuAstral does not promise guaranteed predictions, does not replace professional advice, and does not sell personalized spiritual consultations.

## What maintaining the project means

Maintenance includes:

- reviewing institutional pages, guides, and zodiac sign pages;
- correcting links, navigation errors, and language inconsistencies;
- keeping the interactive reading working in the browser;
- explaining how local data, cookies, and advertising may be used;
- preserving separation between editorial content and monetization.

## Review routine

Pages are updated when there is a relevant change in text, structure, policy, methodology, or navigation. Small spelling fixes may happen without a separate note, but changes that affect interpretation or trust should be reflected in `lastmod`.

## Relationship with advertising

MeuAstral may be funded by ads. Maintenance aims to keep advertising identifiable and separate from editorial content. Learn more in [How MeuAstral is funded](/en/how-meuastral-is-funded/).

## How to get in touch

For corrections, questions, and suggestions, use [contato@meuastral.com](mailto:contato@meuastral.com) or read the [Contact](/en/contact/) page.
