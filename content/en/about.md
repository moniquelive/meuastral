MeuAstral is a free self-knowledge tool. It brings together a daily horoscope, biorhythm cycles, and an ascended master reading based on your birth date.

The site is designed for reflection and entertainment. It is not a substitute for medical, psychological, financial, legal, or professional advice.

The interactive tool runs in your browser. When you save a birth date, it is stored locally on your own device so the reading can load again later.

## What you can find on the site

MeuAstral combines evergreen editorial pages with an interactive reading. The pages explain concepts, limits, and reading approaches. The interactive tool uses the selected date to show zodiac sign, biorhythm cycles, and a symbolic ascended master reading.

This combination helps separate context from result. Instead of treating a daily sentence as an order, the site suggests questions: what theme deserves attention, what rhythm fits the day, and what choice can be made with more awareness.

## How to use it responsibly

Use MeuAstral as symbolic language. If a reading mentions rest, communication, or change, treat it as a prompt for observation, not as an obligation. Important decisions still depend on facts, limits, honest conversation, and qualified guidance when needed.

It can also help to compare readings over time. A single text may stand out because of the emotion of the moment; repeated patterns are usually more useful for reflection.

## Transparency and maintenance

The project is maintained through content review, technical updates, and institutional pages that explain methodology, editorial policy, privacy, and funding. When advertising is present, it should remain separate from editorial interpretation.
