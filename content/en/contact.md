The official MeuAstral contact channel is email. It exists for navigation questions, content correction requests, guide suggestions, and technical issue reports.

- **Email:** [contato@meuastral.com](mailto:contato@meuastral.com)

## When to use this channel

Use contact for topics such as:

- spelling, link, or information corrections on editorial pages;
- questions about methodology, privacy, terms, or funding;
- suggestions for new self-knowledge guides;
- reports of interactive reading loading issues;
- clarification requests about content limits.

## How to help the review

When writing, include whenever possible:

1. the page link involved;
2. an objective description of the issue or suggestion;
3. browser and device information for technical errors;
4. a screenshot without personal data, if it helps explain the case.

## Response time and scope

Messages are reviewed in arrival order. The project aims to respond within 3 business days, but timing can vary according to volume and complexity.

Contact does not replace medical, psychological, legal, financial, or individual spiritual guidance. MeuAstral publishes symbolic and editorial content for self-knowledge and conscious entertainment.

## Contact privacy

Avoid sending sensitive personal data. Share only what is necessary to explain the question or issue. Data handling follows the [Privacy policy](/en/privacy-policy/).
