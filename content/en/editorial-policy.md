MeuAstral publishes content for entertainment, symbolism, and self-reflection. Editorial pages should be clear about limits and avoid absolute promises.

Our goal is to explain concepts, guide use of the tool, and help readers reflect on energy, mood, focus, and choices for the day.

When daily horoscope data comes from an external provider, that integration is treated as a daily content source, not as a guaranteed prediction.

## Publishing principles

Texts should be useful, readable, and responsible. This means explaining symbolic terms in plain language, avoiding unnecessary fear, and not turning horoscope, biorhythm, or spirituality into a promise of results.

When a page suggests reflection, it should preserve the reader's autonomy. The final choice belongs to the person, especially in matters of health, money, work, relationships, and safety.

## Symbolic content and limits

MeuAstral may discuss energy, cycles, zodiac signs, ascended masters, and archetypes. These themes are treated as cultural, spiritual, or symbolic language, not as diagnosis, professional advice, or a guarantee of the future.

Content that mentions care, pause, action, or conversation should be read as a prompt for observation. It does not replace medical, psychological, legal, financial, or technical support.

## Review and updates

Institutional pages, guides, and sign pages may be reviewed when there is a language correction, clarity improvement, structure change, or policy update. Relevant changes should appear in the page `lastmod` field.

Broken links, translation issues, spelling mistakes, or interpretation concerns can be sent through the [Contact](/en/contact/) page. Maintenance prioritizes corrections that improve trust, accessibility, and clarity.

## Advertising and independence

Advertising should not decide editorial content. Methodology, privacy, and funding pages exist to explain how the project works and to keep symbolic interpretation, the interactive experience, and monetization separate.
