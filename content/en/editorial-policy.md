MeuAstral publishes content for entertainment, symbolism, and self-reflection. Editorial pages should be clear about limits and avoid absolute promises.

Our goal is to explain concepts, guide use of the tool, and help readers reflect on energy, mood, focus, and choices for the day.

When daily horoscope data comes from an external provider, that integration is treated as a daily content source, not as a guaranteed prediction.
