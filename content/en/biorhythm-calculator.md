Biorhythm charts are a symbolic way to look at repeating cycles from your birth date. MeuAstral shows three classic pillars: physical energy, emotional balance, and intellectual focus.

The physical cycle is associated with vitality, stamina, and body rhythm. The emotional cycle is associated with mood, sensitivity, and relationship tone. The intellectual cycle is associated with focus, clarity, and reasoning.

## How to read the cycles

Each cycle is shown as a wave, not as a verdict. A higher point can suggest more available energy in that symbolic area. A lower point can suggest a day for pacing, reducing overload, or choosing simpler actions.

The most useful reading usually comes from comparing cycles. A high intellectual cycle with a lower emotional cycle may favor planning before a difficult conversation. A lower physical cycle may suggest rest even if the mind wants to move quickly.

## Practical use

Use the biorhythm calculator as a daily check-in. Choose your birth date, notice which cycle asks for attention, and turn the result into one practical adjustment: rest, focus, communication, or a smaller task list.

The chart does not measure health, mood, or ability. It is a symbolic rhythm model for reflection and entertainment.
