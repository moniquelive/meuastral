MeuAstral combines three symbolic layers. The zodiac sign is identified through tropical zodiac date ranges. The biorhythm chart calculates physical, emotional, and intellectual cycles from age in days. The ascended master reading uses a numerological reduction of the birth date.

These methods are presented as symbolic language. They do not scientifically measure personality, destiny, or health.

The goal is to provide a simple daily reflection panel.

## Sun sign

The sign is defined by birth date and traditional tropical zodiac ranges. This layer works as a basic astrological reference: element, modality, ruling planet, and recurring themes for each sign.

Because transition dates can vary by year, birth time, and location, MeuAstral treats the result as a general symbolic reference. Anyone who needs full astrological precision should use a birth chart calculated with time and place of birth.

## Biorhythm

Biorhythm uses the number of days lived to represent three classic cycles: physical, emotional, and intellectual. The reading does not measure health, mood, or real ability. It shows a periodic pattern used as a metaphor for energy, sensitivity, and focus.

In practice, the chart helps ask simple questions: does the day call for action or recovery, conversation or retreat, deep study or basic organization?

## Ascended masters and rays

The ascended master reading uses numerological reduction of the birth date to associate the person with a symbolic ray. This layer offers spiritual reflection language, such as qualities, challenges, and development themes.

It does not determine destiny, spiritual level, or religious obligation. The user can accept, adapt, or ignore any association that does not fit their belief or experience.
