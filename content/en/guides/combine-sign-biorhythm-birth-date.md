MeuAstral brings together three layers: sign, biorhythm, and ascended masters. Each one answers a different question. When you understand that difference, the reading becomes clearer and less automatic.

## The sign shows astrological language

The sign organizes themes of identity, rhythm, and way of acting. In the daily reading, it helps interpret the symbolic climate of the day and notice which posture may be more conscious.

Read the sign as an invitation, not as a label. Even when a sentence feels accurate, it remains a point of reflection.

## Biorhythm shows cycles

Biorhythm adds a reading of rhythm. It asks how physical energy, emotional sensitivity, and intellectual clarity appear within the symbolic model of the site.

This layer helps adjust intensity. A suggestion for action may need more calm if the physical cycle is low. A conversation may need more care if the emotional cycle is sensitive.

## Birth date connects the reading with a ray

The ascended master reading uses the birth date to reach a symbolic ray. This result offers a quality of support: faith, wisdom, love, purity, healing, service, or transformation, depending on the ray.

Use this layer as a keyword. It can guide a small practice: breathe before answering, organize one task, care for the body, study, express gratitude, or close a cycle.

## A simple sequence

1. read the sign’s theme;
2. check which biorhythm cycle asks for attention;
3. observe the quality of the spiritual ray;
4. turn everything into one small realistic action.

This sequence prevents over-interpretation. Instead of trying to predict the whole day, you choose one concrete attitude to live with more presence.
