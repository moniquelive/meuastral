In MeuAstral, the ascended master reading connects a birth date with one of seven symbolic rays. Each ray brings a color, a master, an archangel, and a set of qualities for personal reflection.

This reading should be understood as symbolic spiritual language. It does not define destiny, personal value, or a mandatory mission. It serves as an invitation to observe virtues, challenges, and attitudes that may deserve attention during the day.

## What a symbolic ray is

A ray represents a group of themes. The Blue Ray, for example, is associated with faith, power, and action. The Golden Ray is linked with wisdom and illumination. The Pink Ray speaks of love and beauty. Each association helps name a quality in a more imaginative way.

The goal is not to place anyone in a fixed category. The point is to offer an image for meditation: what quality can I develop today? Where does this theme appear in my choices?

## How to use the ascended master

Read the master’s name and description as a point of concentration. If the reading speaks of discipline, ask where your energy needs direction. If it speaks of love, ask where you can act with more kindness. If it speaks of transformation, ask what needs to be reorganized.

Avoid turning the reading into spiritual obligation. A symbol is useful when it expands awareness, not when it increases guilt or fear.

## Relationship with archangels

The associated archangel works as another layer of language. It reinforces the ray’s theme and offers an image of protection, clarity, healing, service, or transformation, depending on the reading.

## How to combine it with horoscope and biorhythm

The ascended master can work as the tone of the day. The horoscope shows an astrological theme; biorhythm shows symbolic cycles; the spiritual ray offers a keyword. Together, they form a fuller question: with what energy can I move through the day more consciously?
