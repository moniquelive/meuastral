Biorhythm is a symbolic way to observe repeated cycles from a birth date. MeuAstral presents three dimensions: physical, emotional, and intellectual. The reading does not measure health or real performance; it offers a reference for reflecting on rhythm.

## Physical cycle

The physical cycle is usually associated with vitality, stamina, body rhythm, and practical energy. When it is high, the reading may suggest more initiative. When it is low, it may invite rest, nourishment, and respect for limits.

This does not mean you are objectively strong or weak. It simply raises a question: is my body being considered in today’s choices?

## Emotional cycle

The emotional cycle relates to mood, sensitivity, bonds, and affective reactions. On high days, there may be more openness to connection and expression. On low days, reducing exposure and avoiding emotion-driven decisions may help.

The point is to observe, not label. A low emotional cycle does not make the day bad; it asks for more care with interpretation, expectation, and communication.

## Intellectual cycle

The intellectual cycle is associated with focus, clarity, reasoning, and mental organization. When it is high, it may support study, planning, and decision-making. When it is low, slower review, less multitasking, and checking important information may be wiser.

Use this cycle as a reminder of method. If the mind is scattered, simplify. If it is clear, structure tasks that require analysis.

## How to combine the cycles

Avoid reading each cycle in isolation. One day may show high physical energy and low emotional sensitivity. Another may show mental clarity while the body asks for rest. The reading becomes more useful when you ask which area needs support.

## Reading limits

Biorhythm does not replace medical, psychological, or professional evaluation. Use it as symbolic self-perception. If something in your body, mood, or mind worries you, seek qualified help.
