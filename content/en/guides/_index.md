MeuAstral guides explain how to use symbolic readings without turning each sentence into a rule. The goal is to help you build a simple ritual: observe the sign, compare the biorhythm cycles, read the spiritual ray, and choose one conscious action for the day.

Each guide starts from a practical question. How can you read a daily horoscope without anxiety? What does a low emotional cycle suggest? How can ascended masters work as reflective language rather than spiritual pressure? How do all three readings fit together?

## How to use these guides

Start with the topic that appears most often in your routine. If you open the horoscope looking for a quick answer, read the daily horoscope guide. If you track energy, mood, and focus during the week, start with biorhythm. If you want to understand spiritual rays, read the ascended masters guide first.

These texts support the interactive reading; they do not replace your own judgment. Use each guide as a method for pausing, interpreting, and acting with less impulse.

## Important limits

No MeuAstral guide replaces medical, psychological, legal, financial, or professional advice. The content is symbolic, editorial, and intended for conscious entertainment.
